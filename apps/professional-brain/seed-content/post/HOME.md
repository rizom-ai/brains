# Welcome

This is your professional knowledge base and portfolio.

Use this space to:

- **Capture Knowledge**: Store notes, ideas, and learnings
- **Showcase Work**: Publish blog posts and portfolio pieces
- **Build in Public**: Share your journey and connect with others

## Getting Started

Edit the content in `seed-content/` to customize:

- Your profile information
- Website presentation
- Initial pages and posts

The content is stored as markdown files that sync bidirectionally with the database, giving you full control over your data.

## What's Next?

Start creating! Everything you add here can be published to your professional website with a single command.
