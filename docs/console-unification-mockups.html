Tokens before chrome: one semantic sheet (--console-*) with two climates — instrument and paper — replaces the per-surface --dashboard-* / --chat-* re-declarations and their fallback chains. Vermilion, verdigris and amber stay one name with climate-resolved values; mono consolidates on JetBrains Mono.
The strip is CSS, not a shared component: ~30 lines of markup, re-rendered natively by each framework (Preact in the SSR dashboard, React in the chat and CMS shells) against the shared stylesheet. No cross-framework component, no runtime coupling; surface links derive from the webserver's route registry.
Sequence: token extraction lands as the first step of dashboard Phase 1 (which ships the strip); the CMS wears the strip from its Phase 0 walking skeleton; chat adopts it as a small standalone change. ⌘K's cross-surface index is the follow-on once all three share the chrome.
