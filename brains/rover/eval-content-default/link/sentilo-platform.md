Barcelona's open source sensor platform. Handles ingestion, storage, and visualization of urban IoT data. Used for air quality, noise, traffic, and water level monitoring. Any city can deploy it. Data feeds public dashboards and academic research.

Key design decision: edge aggregation before cloud storage. Sensors output counts and averages, not raw streams. Privacy by architecture, not policy.
