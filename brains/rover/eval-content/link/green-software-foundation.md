Industry consortium focused on reducing the environmental impact of software. Publishes the Software Carbon Intensity (SCI) specification — a standard way to measure the carbon impact of software systems. Members include Microsoft, Google, Accenture, and Thoughtworks.

Their practitioner guide is the most practical resource I've found for green software engineering.
