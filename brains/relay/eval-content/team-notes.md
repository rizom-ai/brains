# Relay POC team notes

Relay should prove a focused loop before new entity types are added:

1. Capture useful team knowledge from chat, notes, links, and peer-brain records.
2. Summarize conversations into durable memory.
3. Extract topics from existing entities, including draft links.
4. Share a small public knowledge surface only when the preset asks for it.

The team decided not to copy Rover's publishing-heavy stack into Relay. Relay should stay centered on collaborative knowledge and coordination.
